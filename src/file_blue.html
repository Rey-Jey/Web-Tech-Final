Name: Jacqueline Bleu

Age: 20

Height: 5' 2"

Known Crimes:

  • Glowing
  • Hummuscide

Status: Wandering

Overview

Jacqueline Bleu, while a fairly new Wacky Criminal added to the WCD, is responsible for multiple accounts of inconveniences, especially in locations inappropriate for such behavior caused via her Wackness. It is also noted that she is an avid fan of hummus.

Glowing

Jacqueline Bleu has the ability to glow a bright blue light, illuminating everything around her, measured to be around 15ft. While at first seeming harmless, anything illuminated by the blue light begins to inexplicably move slowly towards Bleu, including inanimate objects.

Living organisms also seem to be affected by the blue light, and appear to enter a trance-like state, unable to respond while walking slowly towards Bleu until given physical stimulus. When released from the trance, most victims point out the smell of hummus lingering in their minds.

Hummuscide

Through unknown circumstances, whenever Bleu comes into physical contact with a container of hummus, it somehow gets obliterated, as some witnesses claim. Regardless, the sound created by this phenomena is enough to shatter eardrums, usually leaving victims hospitalized for a couple weeks.

Methods of Capture

When dealing with Jacqueline Bleu, it is noted that the most effective method of luring her is with a small container of hummus. Place the hummus in a location far from both yourself and others in order to prevent the risk of being affected by Hummuscide. For this same reason, using the ROWCC is the safest way to capture her.
