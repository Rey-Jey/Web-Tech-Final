Overview

Jacqueline Bleu, while a fairly new Wacky Criminal added to the WCD, is responsible for multiple accounts of inconveniences, especially in locations inappropriate for such behavior caused via her Wackness.

Glowing

Jacqueline Bleu has the ability to glow a bright blue light, illuminating everything around her, measured to be around 15ft. While at first seeming harmless, anything illuminated by the blue light begins to inexplicably move slowly towards Bleu, including inanimate objects.

Living organisms also seem to be affected by the blue light, and appear to enter a trance-like state, unable to respond and walking slowly towards Bleu until given physical stimulus. When released from the trance, most victims point out the smell of hummus lingering in their minds.
