About the Events Venue

A section of our restaurant is designated for performances! This part of the restaurant is separate from the main dining hall, so don't worry about any inconveniences during your meal!

If you wish to attend a performance while enjoying your meal, please specify Event seating when making your reservation.

Schedule a Performance

The Blind Spot is open to performers! Please contact us one month in advance in order to fill a time slot during the day.

Please let us know about your performance prior to arriving at the venue. Due to previous incidents, the following are currently not accepted at this time:

  • Stand-up Comedy
  • Bands with more than 8 members
  • Interpretive dance
  • Sit-down Comedy
  • Hypnosis
  • Opera
  • Pyrotechnics
  • Lie-down Comedy
  • Live Live-Streaming
Want to perform at our restaurant? Call 1 800-555-9119
