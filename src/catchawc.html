How to Capture a Wacky Criminal

0. By all means, you should always contact our helpline before doing anything

Before you try to capture the Wacky Criminal on your own, please contact a professional: (555) 555-666-6777

1. Identify the Wacky Criminal

There are visuals of most of the Wacky Criminals within the database, Please make sure to identify the criminal before acting.

2. Lure the Criminal

Using information gathered from the WCD, safely lure the Wacky Criminal to an isolated location. Make sure to maintain a safe distance from the criminal to ensure maximum safety.

3. Capture the Criminal

Make sure you have a Radial Oscillating Wacky Criminal Collector (ROWCC). If you don't currently have a ROWCC, DO NOT engage the criminal, await help from a professional WCD specialist. Furthermore, ensure that the ROWCC will be an effective tool. Not all Wacky Criminals can be captured using the ROWCC.

If you do have a ROWCC however. Maintain a safe distance from the Wacky Criminal before activating the ROWCC, then throw it at the Wacky Criminal. Make sure the ROWCC hits them. For the highest success rate, aim for the center of the Wacky Criminal's chest.

The ROWCC retains the captured criminal for 2 days before releasing them.

4. Turn in the Criminal

Every street has a Wacky Criminal Collection Station. Please make your way to one of these stations as soon as you've captured the Wacky Criminal in order to reduce risk of the criminal escaping again.
